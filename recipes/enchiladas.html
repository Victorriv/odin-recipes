Enchiladas

Description

This dish is a delicious tradition Hispanic meal. Enchiladas originated from Mexico, where the practice of rolling tortillas around other food dates back at least to Aztec times. Corn was a staple crop at the time and corn tortillas were common. It is a soft tortilla wrapped around a filling dn covered in sauce,then baked or broiled. The word Enchilada comes from the Spanish word enchilada, which means "to season with chili".

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Protein of choice
  • 1lb of Tomatillos(green tomatoes)
  • 2 Serrano chili
  • 2 Jalapeno
  • Slice of Onion
  • Cilantro
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Once boiled, add the tomatillos, jalapeno, and serrano chili
  3. Then you add all the boiled ingredients into a blender and blend them
  4. Then you add the slice of onion with cilantro and garlic into the blender and blend that as well
  5. Then you salt to taste
  6. Next heat up a pan with oil and pour everything from the blender into the pan and stir it. If its too thick then add a bit of water.
  7. Roll your tortillas with your choice of protein and drench it with the sauce.
