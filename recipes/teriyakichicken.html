Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Teriyaki chicken is chicken coated in a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce, which is a glaze made from soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. The term "teriyaki chicken" can refer to chicken prepared using any cooking method, but the dish originated from a Japanese cooking technique where meat is grilled or broiled after being marinated in the glaze

Ingredients

  • 5oz Chicken
  • 1/3 cup Brocoli
  • 1 cup White Rice

Steps

  1. Grill the chicken for 2 mins on each side
  2. Broil the brocoli for 5 mins
  3. boil the rice in a cup of water for 15mins
